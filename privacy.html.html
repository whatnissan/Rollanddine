Privacy Policy

Roll & Dine

Roll & Dine ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

Location Data: Roll & Dine accesses your device's location to find restaurants near you. This location data is used only in real-time to perform searches and is never stored, saved, or transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To find and display restaurants near your current location
  • To calculate distances to nearby restaurants
  • To provide directions via Apple Maps

Data Storage

Roll & Dine does not store any personal data. We do not have servers or databases that collect your information. All searches are performed locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Roll & Dine uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple MapKit: To search for restaurants and display maps. Apple's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • Apple Maps: To provide directions when you choose to navigate to a restaurant.

No Account Required

Roll & Dine does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information.

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or advertising frameworks. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Children's Privacy

Roll & Dine does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us
